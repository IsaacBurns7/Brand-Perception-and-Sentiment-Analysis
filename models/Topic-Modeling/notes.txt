===========================================================================
TOPIC MODELING COMPARISON: LDA VS. BERTOPIC
===========================================================================

METRIC            LDA (Statistical)             BERTopic (Neural/Clustering)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Doc Length        Longer is better. Needs       Short to Mid-range. Struggles 
                  high word co-occurrence.      with >512 tokens (BERT limit).

Doc Count         Needs 1k+ documents to        Works with hundreds; 
                  avoid "noise" topics.         thrives at 10k-100k.

Scaling (N)       Very efficient. Handles       Bottlenecked by UMAP/HDBSCAN.
                  millions of docs on CPU.      Usually requires a GPU.

Preprocessing     Aggressive (Lemmatization,    Lax (Needs context, 
                  Stopword removal).            stopwords, and structure).

Noise Tolerance   Low. Sensitive to high-       High. Transformers handle 
                  frequency generic words.      polysemy and noise well.

Output Type       Probabilistic distribution    Centroid-based clustering
                  (Topic-Word / Doc-Topic).     (Document-Topic).

===========================================================================
SCALING EXPECTATIONS
===========================================================================

* LDA Performance: Best for large archives (N > 10,000) with long, formal 
  documents. Scales linearly.
  
* BERTopic Performance: Best for short-form text or semantic granularity. 
  Performance is tied to the quality of the embedding model used.
