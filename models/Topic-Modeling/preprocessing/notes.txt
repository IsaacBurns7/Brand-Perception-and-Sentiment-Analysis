Preprocessing NLP data for LDA has the following stages: 
1. Load/ingest data
2. Cleaning/normalization
- Convert to lowercase
- Remove URLs, HTML tags, emojis, special characterize
- Normalize whitespace
- Optionally remove numbers
3. Tokenization
- Split document of text into words(tokens)
4. Stopword removal
- Remove high-frequency common words that give little information 
5. Lemmatization / Stemming
- running/runs/ran -> run
6. Phrase detection
- Bigrams and Trigrams
7. Filtering(optional)
- Extremely rare or common words removed
    - appearing in fewer than min_docs documents
    - appearing in more than max_doc_proportion
    - dictionary.filter_extremes in gensim
8. Build dictionary and corpus 
- token lists to numeric representation for LDA
- corpus is a bag of words(word,freq) per document
9. TF-IDF Weighting(optional)
- Reduce weight of common words to improve LDA topics
10. Stage inspection and logging
- Save articles and print metrics for debugging 

Refactor LDA_normalize_corpus into these 10 stages, each with a file of their own, and then aggregated via main.py
